## v1.3 - "The Marksman's Hammer" (December 29, 2025)

### Added
- Kasrkin (10 models) - Elite snipers with Hot-shot Marksman Rifle
- Rogal Dorn Battle Tank - T12, W18, multi-profile turret
- Scout Sentinels - Scouts 9" for early board control
- Triple Sniper Doctrine tactical section
- Model Assembly Guides for all points levels

### Changed
- Drill Commander enhancement replaces Grand Strategist
- 1000pt list redesigned with Kasrkin + Scout Sentinels
- Sniper progression: 500pt (5 shots) → 1000pt (6 shots) → 2000pt (12 shots)

### Removed
- Leman Russ Punisher (replaced by Rogal Dorn)
- Armoured Sentinels (replaced by Scout Sentinels)
- Extra Command Squad and Chimera (to fund upgrades)
