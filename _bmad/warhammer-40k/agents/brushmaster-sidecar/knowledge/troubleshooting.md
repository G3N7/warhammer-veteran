# Painting Troubleshooting Guide

Quick fixes for common painting problems.

---

## Paint Problems

### Problem: Paint too thick
**Symptoms:** Brushstrokes visible, chunky texture, obscured detail
**Causes:**
- Not enough water/medium
- Old, dried-out paint
- Too much paint on brush

**Solutions:**
1. Add water (start with 1:1 ratio)
2. Use flow improver medium
3. Wipe excess on palette
4. Replace old paints

---

### Problem: Paint too thin
**Symptoms:** Runs into recesses, no coverage, takes forever
**Causes:**
- Too much water
- Over-thinned paint

**Solutions:**
1. Let current coat dry completely
2. Use less water next coat
3. Build up with more layers
4. Add medium instead of pure water

---

### Problem: Chalky finish
**Symptoms:** Matte, dusty texture on dried paint
**Causes:**
- Paint dried on brush before application
- Hot/dry environment
- Incompatible paint/primer

**Solutions:**
1. Use wet palette
2. Work faster
3. Add retarder medium
4. Mist model with water occasionally
5. Apply thin glaze of same color to smooth

---

### Problem: Coffee staining
**Symptoms:** Ring of pigment around edge of thinned paint
**Causes:**
- Too much water, not enough medium
- Paint separating

**Solutions:**
1. Use medium instead of water
2. Add matte medium to paint
3. Work paint into surface more

---

### Problem: Glossy spots
**Symptoms:** Shiny patches in otherwise matte finish
**Causes:**
- Paint pooled and dried thick
- Varnish contamination
- Some colors naturally glossy

**Solutions:**
1. Apply matte varnish
2. Thin paint more
3. Remove pooled paint while wet

---

## Brush Problems

### Problem: Brush fraying/splitting
**Symptoms:** Bristles spread out, won't hold point
**Causes:**
- Paint drying in ferrule
- Poor cleaning
- Bad brush quality

**Solutions:**
1. Clean properly after EVERY session
2. Use brush soap/conditioner
3. Never let paint dry in brush
4. Don't leave brush in water tip-down

---

### Problem: Brush losing bristles
**Symptoms:** Hairs coming out while painting
**Causes:**
- Old/cheap brush
- Dried paint in ferrule
- Aggressive cleaning

**Solutions:**
1. Replace brush
2. Deep clean with brush restorer
3. Be gentle when cleaning

---

### Problem: Can't get fine lines
**Symptoms:** Lines too thick, no control
**Causes:**
- Wrong brush size
- Too much paint
- Brush not coming to point

**Solutions:**
1. Use smaller brush
2. Less paint on brush
3. Reshape brush with saliva/water
4. Check brush isn't damaged

---

## Primer Problems

### Problem: Primer too thick
**Symptoms:** Detail obscured, textured surface
**Causes:**
- Too close when spraying
- Too much primer
- Humidity issues

**Solutions:**
1. Strip and re-prime
2. Light sanding (risky)
3. Next time: thin coats, 8-12" distance

---

### Problem: Primer won't stick
**Symptoms:** Beading, flaking primer
**Causes:**
- Mold release residue on model
- Greasy surface
- Wrong primer for material

**Solutions:**
1. Wash model in soapy water
2. Scrub with toothbrush
3. Rinse and dry completely
4. Use appropriate primer for material

---

### Problem: Frosting/fuzzy primer
**Symptoms:** White, textured, powdery finish
**Causes:**
- High humidity
- Too far away when spraying
- Cold temperature

**Solutions:**
1. Strip and re-prime
2. Prime indoors, controlled environment
3. Check humidity (<60%) and temp (>50°F/10°C)

---

## Color Problems

### Problem: Colors look muddy
**Symptoms:** Dull, grey-ish, lifeless
**Causes:**
- Mixing too many colors
- Wrong complementary choices
- Over-washed

**Solutions:**
1. Use purer colors
2. Limit mixing to 2-3 paints
3. Add contrast with highlights
4. Glaze with pure color to revive

---

### Problem: Can't match a color
**Symptoms:** Touch-ups visible, inconsistent
**Causes:**
- Paint dried differently
- Mixed color not recorded
- Old paint changed

**Solutions:**
1. Record all mix recipes
2. Mix large batches for projects
3. Feather edges of touch-ups
4. Glaze over to unify

---

### Problem: White/Yellow coverage issues
**Symptoms:** Patchy, takes many coats
**Causes:**
- These pigments are naturally translucent

**Solutions:**
1. Undercoat with light grey (for white)
2. Undercoat with pink/bone (for yellow)
3. Use "base" paints not "layer"
4. Accept 4-5 thin coats minimum

## Quick Fixes

| Problem | Quick Fix |
|---------|-----------|
| Small mistake | Paint over while wet |
| Dried mistake | Carefully scrape with knife edge |
| Missed spot | Touch up with thin coat |
| Pooled wash | Wick away with damp brush |
| Fingerprint | Let dry, paint over |
| Dust in paint | Pick out with needle |
| Dropped model | Deep breaths, touch up |
| Paint on wrong area | Fix immediately if wet, or paint over |

## Prevention Checklist

Before painting:
- [ ] Model cleaned and primed
- [ ] Paints shaken
- [ ] Brushes clean and in good condition
- [ ] Good lighting
- [ ] Wet palette prepared
- [ ] Water cup fresh

During painting:
- [ ] Thin your paints
- [ ] Check brush frequently
- [ ] Take breaks
- [ ] Good ventilation

After painting:
- [ ] Clean brushes properly
- [ ] Store brushes tip-up
- [ ] Close paint pots
- [ ] Admire your work!
