## Meta Trends

### Current Dominant Strategies
1. **Mobile Objective Play** - Fast units scoring primaries
2. **Character Hammer** - Buff stacking on key characters
3. **Monster Mash** - Multiple large models with good saves
4. **Elite Infantry** - Small, tough, killy units

### Counters to Watch
- Anti-tank is essential (melta, lascannon)
- Mortal wound output valuable
- Deep strike denial important
- Objective secured bodies matter
