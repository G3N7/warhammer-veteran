# Space Wolves Terminator Army - Tactical Reference

**Faction:** Space Wolves (Adeptus Astartes)
**Detachment:** Saga of the Beastslayer (Lethal Hits vs Characters/Monsters/Vehicles)
**Theme:** Terminator-heavy elite force led by legendary heroes

## Army Lists

### 500pt - "Wolf Pack"

| Unit | Models | Role | Points |
|------|--------|------|--------|
| Arjac Rockfist | 1 | Warlord, leads 10-man | 95 |
| Enhancement: Hunter's Instincts | - | On Arjac | 20 |
| Wolf Guard Terminators | 10 | Primary Battle Line (Arjac) | 340 |
| Fenrisian Wolves | 5 | Screening | 40 |
| **TOTAL** | **16** | | **495** |

**Tactical Overview:**
- Single 10-man Terminator brick with Arjac
- No Deep Strike - all units start on board
- Castle center objective, wolves screen front
- Limited mobility and board control (15 models total)
- Incredibly durable but no redundancy

**How to Play:**
1. Deploy wolves 9" forward to screen charges
2. Castle on center with maximum fire lanes
3. Use Oath of Moment each turn on priority targets
4. Advance slowly (6" + Advance if needed)
5. Save CP for defensive Stratagems

| Unit | Models | Role | Points |
|------|--------|------|--------|
| Logan Grimnar | 1 | Warlord, leads 10-man | 110 |
| Enhancement: Blade of the Slayer | - | On Logan | 25 |
| Arjac Rockfist | 1 | Leads 5-man | 95 |
| Wolf Guard Terminators | 10 | Primary Castle (Logan) | 340 |
| Wolf Guard Terminators | 5 | Objective Holders (Arjac) | 170 |
| Wolf Guard Terminators | 5 | Deep Strike Alpha | 170 |
| Fenrisian Wolves | 10 | Screening | 80 |
| **TOTAL** | **32** | | **990** |

**Tactical Overview:**
- Two castle blocks (Logan's 10-man + Arjac's 5-man) hold center
- One 5-man Deep Strikes Turn 2+
- Both legendary heroes on field
- No CP generation (no Bjorn)
- No +1 to charge (no Venerable Dread)

**How to Play:**
1. **Turn 1:** Deploy Logan's 10-man + Arjac's 5-man on center objectives. Wolves screen.
2. **Turn 2:** Deep Strike 5-man into enemy backfield or undefended objective
3. **Turn 3+:** Logan's brick advances, Arjac's 5-man defends home base
4. **Target Priority:** Use Blade of Slayer (Precision) + Beastslayer (Lethal Hits) to snipe Characters
5. **CP Management:** No Bjorn = start with 3CP, save for critical moments

### 2000pt - "Wolves of the Fang"

| Unit | Models | Role | Points |
|------|--------|------|--------|
| Logan Grimnar | 1 | Warlord, leads 10-man | 110 |
| Enhancement: Blade of the Slayer | - | On Logan | 25 |
| Arjac Rockfist | 1 | Leads 10-man | 95 |
| Enhancement: Hunter's Instincts | - | On Arjac | 20 |
| Bjorn the Fell-Handed | 1 | CP Generator / Fire Support | 170 |
| Venerable Dreadnought | 1 | Melee Support / Charge Aura | 140 |
| Wolf Guard Terminators | 10 | Primary Castle (Logan) | 340 |
| Wolf Guard Terminators | 10 | Secondary Castle (Arjac) | 340 |
| Wolf Guard Terminators | 10 | Backfield Holders | 340 |
| Blood Claws | 10 | Aggressive Melee | 140 |
| Grey Hunters | 10 | Mid-field Flex | 140 |
| Fenrisian Wolves | 10 | Screening / Fast Grab | 80 |
| **TOTAL** | **64** | | **1,940** |

**60pts remaining for wargear/additional units**

**Tactical Overview:**
- Maximum legal Terminators (30 models) under Rule of Three
- Two 10-man castle blocks led by Logan + Arjac
- Bjorn generates +1CP per turn
- Venerable Dread gives +1 to Advance/Charge within 6"
- Power armor support (Blood Claws + Grey Hunters) adds model count and melee pressure

**How to Play:**
1. **Deployment:** Logan's 10-man + Arjac's 10-man on center objectives. Third 10-man holds backfield. Blood Claws mid-field for counter-charge. Grey Hunters on flank. Wolves screen. Dreads positioned for auras.
2. **Turn 1:** Castle up, activate Oath of Moment, Bjorn provides fire support
3. **Turn 2:** Blood Claws charge or screen. Grey Hunters hold mid-field. Terminators advance with Dread aura.
4. **Turn 3+:** Grind opponents down through superior durability. Hold 3+ objectives.
5. **Win Condition:** Preserve Terminators, trade power armor efficiently, dominate objectives late game

**CP Generation:** Bjorn gives +1CP per turn = sustainable Stratagem usage

### Logan Grimnar

**Stats:** M5" | T5 | Sv2+/4++ | W6 | Ld5+ | OC1 | 110pts

**Wargear:**
- The Axe Morkai (melee)
- Storm bolter

**Special Rules:**
- Epic Hero (only 1 per army)
- Can lead Wolf Guard Terminators
- Leader abilities boost attached unit

**Why Take Logan:** Chapter Master buffs, excellent melee with Axe Morkai, receives Blade of the Slayer enhancement for Precision (Character sniping)

---

### Arjac Rockfist

**Stats:** M5" | T6 | Sv2+/4++ | W5 | Ld6+ | OC1 | 95pts

**Wargear:**
- Foehammer (melee + throwable)
- Storm shield

**Special Rules:**
- Epic Hero (only 1 per army)
- Can lead Wolf Guard Terminators
- Champion of the Kingsguard ability

**Why Take Arjac:** T6 durability, excellent melee damage, receives Hunter's Instincts for reroll wounds vs Characters/Monsters/Vehicles

---

### Bjorn the Fell-Handed

**Stats:** M9" | T9 | Sv2+/5++ | W8 | Ld6+ | OC3 | 170pts

**Wargear:**
- Choose one: Assault cannon OR Helfrost cannon OR Multi-melta
- Heavy flamer
- Trueclaw (S12 AP-2 D3 Lethal Hits)

**Special Rules:**
- **Ancient Tactician:** Gain +1CP at start of each Command phase
- **Legendary Tenacity:** -1 to wound when S > T
- Feel No Pain 5+
- Deadly Demise 1

**Why Take Bjorn:** Free CP every turn, extremely durable, excellent melee and ranged damage, lore-accurate with Terminators

---

### Venerable Dreadnought

**Stats:** M8" | T9 | Sv2+/4++ | W8 | Ld6+ | OC3 | 140pts

**Wargear (Recommended):**
- Fenrisian greataxe (S14 AP-3 D6)
- Blizzard shield (4++ invuln)
- Heavy flamer

**Special Rules:**
- **Fervour of the Ancients (Aura):** +1 to Advance and Charge rolls for SPACE WOLVES within 6"
- Oath of Moment

**Why Take It:** +1 to charge aura is CRITICAL for advancing Terminators and making 9" Deep Strike charges easier. Blizzard shield gives 4++ durability. Fenrisian greataxe hits like a truck.
